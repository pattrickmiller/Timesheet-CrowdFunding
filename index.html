ABOUT TIMESHEET

The purpose of this project is to develop a software that allows managing the resource allocation process based on daily activities by users.

The visualization of how resources are allocated by type of activity, seasonality of allocation (end of month, end of year, projects) optimization points (excess of meetings, excess of informality) allow both a better planning and More effective teams.

Managers need to control how much time has been spent on resources and internal costs with each activity to manage their resources in the best way and get better results.
